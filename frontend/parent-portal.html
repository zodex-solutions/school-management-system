How Parents Can Use the Portal

1
Share the Portal Link

Share the parent portal link with parents via WhatsApp, SMS or email. They can open it on their phone's browser.

2
Parent Self-Registration

Parents register with their name, email, phone and their child's admission number. The system automatically links them to their child.

3
What Parents Can See
  • ✅ Daily attendance of their child
  • 💰 Fee dues and payment history
  • 📝 Homework assignments
  • 📊 Exam results and report cards
  • 📢 School notices and events
  • ✉️ Send messages to teachers/admin
4
Multiple Children Support

Parents with multiple children can link all of them during registration by entering multiple admission numbers (comma separated). They can switch between children in the portal.
